Fried Chicken

Description

The traditional Masaai Chicken.

The fried chicken is cripsy on the outside with a flavourful crust that contrasts and complements its tenderness.

Ingredients

The following are the ingredients that brings its taste:

  • Full Chicken
  • Buttermilk
  • 1 cup cooking oil
  • 2 tablespoon of garlic
  • 1 onion
  • 2 teaspoons cayernne pepper
  • 5 tablespoon of salt

Steps

  1. Prepare the chicken, remove the stomach materials and wash properly
  2. Hang it over the fire until it dries
  3. Preheat the oil in frying pan, put in the chicken. Fry it side by side until it turns little bit brown and tender
  4. Once its brown, remove it and place on a plate
  5. Incase you need it into pieces, cut the chicken into pieces. Add onions, garlic and pepper into the remaining oil in the pan
  6. Apply Salt and serve
  7. Enjoy with delicacies of choice or favorite drinks
