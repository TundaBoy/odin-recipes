Fried Meat

Description

Picture thinly sliced beef,seasoned to perfection, coated in a cripsy golden crust.

With each bite, Experience the satisfying crunch followed by tender, flavorful beef

Ingredients

The following ingredients were used to prepare its taste:

  • 500g meat
  • 2 large tomatoes
  • 1 large onion
  • 1/4 cup cooking oil
  • 2 tea spoon sauce
  • 2 pieces garlic
  • Dania
  • 1/4 tea spoon salt

Steps

  1. Boil the meat for 20 mites in 1 cup of water until it dries
  2. Cut the onion and tomatoes, garlic and Dania separately as the meat boils
  3. When the meat has dried, add cooking oil and stir gently until its color changes to golden brown
  4. Add onions, garlic and continue to stir until onions turns brown and mix completely with the meat
  5. Add tomatoes and Dania into the meat, stir for 1 minute and close the pot with a lid for 2 minutes
  6. Add the salt, sauce and stir to mix the flavors all together. Let it cook for for 2 minutes and serve
